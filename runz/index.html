Press Kit

RUNZ

A roguelite arena shooter where you roll for bullets to build your magazine.

Steam Demo itch.iO Download Kit Discord

Assemble the perfect magazine.

Survive twenty rounds. Between each one, shop for bullets to draft your perfect magazine.  Manage your economy, buy crazy mods, enchant your magazine slots,  carefully place the bullets  in your magazine and let your build loose on your enemies.

  • 20-round survival (planned endless and progression in the full game)
  • Auto-battler style synergy hunting
  • Collect 3 copies of the same bullet to upgrade it
  • Enchant slots to make any bullet inherit a new synergy
  • Mods that can radically change how you approach your run
