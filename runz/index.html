Press Kit

RUNZ

A top-down roguelite arena shooter where every bullet in your magazine matters.

Steam Demo itch.io Builds Download Kit Discord

Your magazine is the build.

Survive twenty rounds. Between each one you're in the shop, rebuilding a twelve-slot magazine out of whatever you can afford — raw bullets, three-of-a-kind fused into starred upgrades, mods bolted on top — then you drop back into the arena and find out if it holds.

There's no separate loadout screen. The magazine is the build, and it's only ever as good as your last trip to the shop.

  • 20-round survival run structure
  • Economy management
  • Collect 3 copies of the same bullet to upgrade it
  • Auto-battler style synergy
  • Mods that can radically change how you approach your run
