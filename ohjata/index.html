OHJATA

Open Steam Screenshots

A redirect-first survivors-like.

Ohjata is a released Action/Indie game built around a unique redirect mechanic: steer your projectile through the arena instead of just firing it.

Upgrades, synergies, gems, and a hand-crafted volcanic map turn each attempt into a different shape — and each death into a choice about what to risk.

Steam Features

Feature names follow the official Steam page.

Redirect Your Weapon

Master the weapon redirect mechanic, improve it during a run, and avoid losing the projectile to walls or off-screen reloads.

Upgrade Through The Run

Level-up choices can reshape a build through weapon upgrades, stat buffs, synergies, random events, and relics.

Gems

Gems can be gathered during runs, spent on powerful mods, dropped on death, and recovered or secured through deposit boxes.

Handcrafted Map

Explore a volcanic landscape, push through harder eras, solve puzzles, read lore, and search for secret bosses.
